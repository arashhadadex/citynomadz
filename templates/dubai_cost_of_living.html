Cost of Living in Dubai

Accommodation

Rent in Dubai is significantly higher than in many other cities. A one-bedroom apartment in a central area can range from $1,500-$3,000 USD per month, while outside the center it might be $800-$1,500 USD.

Food

Dining out can be expensive, especially in upscale restaurants. A meal at a casual restaurant might cost $15-$30 USD. Groceries are also generally more expensive than in many other countries.

Transportation

Public transportation (metro, buses) is efficient but can be more costly than in other cities. Taxis are readily available but add up quickly. Many residents opt for ride-sharing services or personal cars.

Entertainment and Leisure

Dubai offers a luxurious lifestyle with many high-end entertainment options, which can be very costly. However, there are also many free or low-cost activities available.

Overall

Dubai is known for its high cost of living, particularly for accommodation and luxury goods. Careful budgeting is essential for those planning to live here.
