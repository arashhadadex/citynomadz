Cost of Living in Yerevan

Accommodation

Rent for a one-bedroom apartment in the city center can range from $300-$600 USD per month, while outside the center it might be $200-$400 USD. Utilities are generally affordable.

Food

Eating out is relatively inexpensive. A meal at a local restaurant can cost $5-$10 USD. Groceries are also budget-friendly, especially if you buy local produce from markets.

Transportation

Public transportation (metro, buses, minibuses) is very cheap, with a single ride costing around $0.20 USD. Taxis are also affordable for short distances.

Entertainment and Leisure

Enjoying Yerevan's vibrant nightlife, cafes, and cultural events won't break the bank. A coffee might cost $1-$2 USD, and a cinema ticket around $4-$6 USD.

Overall

Yerevan offers a very comfortable and affordable lifestyle, making it an attractive destination for digital nomads and long-term travelers.
